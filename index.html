About Trixi-GPU

Trixi-GPU offers GPU acceleration for solving hyperbolic PDEs. The core solvers are based on the Trixi-Framework, with its acceleration powered by JuliaGPU. Currently, NVIDIA CUDA serves as our primary but experimental GPU acceleration support, and other types of GPU support will be implemented in the future.
